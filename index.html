Backyard Botanicals is a small landscape design company that offers landscape design, plant procurement, installation and seasonal maintenance for residential and commercial properties. We specialize in the use of perennial plants, trees, and shrubs that are native to this region of Kentucky. Our designs are intuitive, vibrant and personalized. We have a deep passion for native plants and the healthy ecology of our community. It is our hope that through our work, we can share this passion with others by putting native plants in backyard gardens across the city.

Whether you want to add to an existing garden or create a whole new look for your overall landscape, we can help you. If you are interested in talking to us about your project, we would love to hear from you.
